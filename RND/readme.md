# Research and Development Head 

## Task

Build an internal tool prototype for Enigma that solves a real operational or technical problem faced by the club or its members.

The goal is not to build a huge production-level platform. The goal is to test whether the candidate can identify a useful problem, research possible solutions, build a working prototype, document it clearly, and explain how it can be expanded into a club-level project.

Enigma regularly conducts workshops, technical events, project initiatives, and branch-based activities. As the club grows, it needs better internal systems for managing members, events, resources, projects, tasks, and communication. Your task is to build a small but functional internal tool that can help Enigma operate better.

You may choose one of the suggested ideas below or propose your own.

### Success Criteria
- Define a clear problem statement or hypothesis.
- Compare approaches or test an idea with measurable results.
- Present findings in a structured way, such as observations, results, and conclusions.
- Show the ability to explain the methodology and reasoning behind the experiment.
- Provide documentation that would allow another person to understand and repeat the work.

### Technical Demo Video
A short technical demo video is necessary for this task. The video should explain the idea, show the experiment or prototype in action, and summarize the findings. During the interview, we will ask about your research process, choices, and how you would teach the concept to someone who wants to recreate the work from scratch.
